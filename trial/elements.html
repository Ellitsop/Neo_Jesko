About us

Koenigsegg, a Swedish avant-garde megacar company founded by Christian von Koenigsegg, is not just about building cars; it's about relentlessly pushing the boundaries of automotive technology and performance. From its humble beginnings in 1994, Koenigsegg has evolved into a global icon, crafting some of the most exclusive and innovative hypercars on the planet. Their philosophy revolves around uncompromising innovation, meticulously engineering every component to achieve unparalleled speed, design, and a driving experience that truly redefines what's possible.
