Jesko

The heart of the Koenigsegg Jesko is a re-designed 5.0-liter twin-turbo V8 engine. This powerhouse delivers an astonishing 1280 horsepower on standard gasoline, escalating to a colossal 1600 horsepower when fueled by E85 biofuel. It features the world's lightest V8 crankshaft, enabling an incredible 8500 rpm redline, and is paired with Koenigsegg's revolutionary 9-speed Light Speed Transmission for near-instantaneous shifts.

The Jesko's aerodynamics are relentlessly functional, built for extreme performance. The Attack variant features a massive active rear wing and an enlarged front splitter with active flaps, generating incredible downforce (up to 1400 kg for the Attack). The Absolut, conversely, features a sleek, elongated tail and removed wing, optimized for minimal drag (Cd 0.278) to achieve record-breaking top speeds.

While highly functional, the Jesko's design remains unmistakably Koenigsegg. It retains the signature wrap-around windscreen and fluid lines, evolving the brand's aesthetic with a more aggressive stance. Every curve and vent serves a purpose, balancing raw visual impact with highly optimized airflow, creating a form that is both beautiful and brutally efficient.
